About Us

Our mission is to tackle ocean plastic pollution, addressing both surface-level and deep-sea waste through innovative, sustainable solutions.

Our Solution

We envision a fleet of self-automated submarines equipped with AI, quantum computing, and sustainable fuel systems to efficiently collect ocean plastic waste.

Problem Statement

Over 11 million tonnes of plastic sit on the ocean floor. With plastic use expected to double by 2040, sustainable solutions are urgently needed.

Our Approach

Combining AI, quantum computing, and clean energy to create an efficient and impactful solution for ocean cleanup.

Key Features

Hybrid Fuel System

Powered by hydrogen fuel cells and solar energy for sustainable and efficient operations.

AI-Optimized Navigation

Real-time AI and quantum computing for optimal waste collection routes.

Advanced Waste Collection

Innovative nets and mechanical arms designed to efficiently collect plastic waste.

Our Impact

Our submarines have the potential to revolutionize ocean cleanup efforts, significantly reducing plastic pollution and preserving marine ecosystems.
